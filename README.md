# IPL Data Analysis

Exploratory analysis of IPL cricket data spanning 16 seasons (2008–2024), 
covering 1,095 matches and 260,920 ball-by-ball deliveries. The analysis 
looks at team performance over the years, top batters by runs and strike rate, 
and top bowlers by wickets and economy.

## Dataset
matches.csv — 1,095 IPL matches with team, toss, winner, venue and result details
deliveries.csv — 260,920 ball by ball deliveries with batter, bowler and run details

## Tools Used
Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib

## Key Findings
Mumbai Indians lead all-time wins with 144, followed by CSK with 138 wins
CSK has the highest win percentage among established teams at 58.2%
V Kohli is the top run scorer with 8,014 runs at a strike rate of 128.5
AD Russell has the highest strike rate among consistent batters at 164.2
SP Narine is the most economical consistent bowler with 200 wickets at an economy of 6.76
Toss decisions and batting order were also analysed across seasons
